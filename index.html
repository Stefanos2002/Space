The Vanguard Syndicate Alliance

The Vanguard Syndicate is an alliance of player owned virtual corporations in EVE Online game.

We are mostly EU, new–player–friendly, real–life–first, nullsec alliance, founded by a group of long–time friends with the goal of creating a home for players and corporations who want to enjoy their time in New Eden on their own terms.

We strive to create a haven free from the grasp of oppressive overlords, forced play styles, mandatory mining ops and taxation for the sake of “leadership” wallets. Our pilots’ freedom and enjoyment come first, and providing room to grow and pursue the gameplay they enjoy is our objective.

Recruitment

For the casual player we offer a peaceful corner of valuable, well–maintained space to flourish and farm away, while enjoying the company on comms. Several of our core members are hardened veterans, with years of experience to share and many stories to tell.

For the more hardcore, we offer the chance to contribute as much as you see fit in any field, from participating in our own and our allies’ fleets to supplying the local markets.

We embrace all who are willing to learn, contribute and be part of the family that we’ve built over the years, and share in the bond of trust we have formed.

Join our Discord, ping a @Recruiter and we can have a chat.
